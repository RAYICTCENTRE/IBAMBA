Our Story

Good food, warm hearts, and Ugandan spirit

Ibamba started with a simple idea: to give people tasty food and a cozy place to gather. We are a family-friendly restaurant in Kampala that serves fresh local meals, cold drinks, and friendly smiles.

Our name means "welcome" in the local spirit — and that's exactly what we offer. Every dish is made with love, using fresh ingredients from Ugandan farmers. From our famous matoke to grilled tilapia, we bring the true taste of home.

We open every day from 7 AM to midnight. Whether you want breakfast, lunch, dinner, or just a coffee break, we are here for you. Come enjoy our space, meet new friends, and taste the best of Uganda.
